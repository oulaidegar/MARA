MARA Vision & Mission
👁️

Vision Statement

To foster a MENA region where technological sovereignty and algorithmic advancement are inextricably bound to human dignity, transparency, and civil equity. We envision a future where regional AI ecosystems—from Cairo to Abu Dhabi—are not merely measured by their compute capacity or capital reserves, but by the robustness of their judicial guardrails, the preservation of their cultural identities, and the guaranteed protection of their citizens' digital rights.

🎯

Mission Statement

MARA exists to democratize access to critical tech intelligence in the Middle East and North Africa. By providing an open-access, bilingual, and bidirectional research archive, we bridge the gap between official state-led AI initiatives and independent civil society critiques. We track physical infrastructure, corporate capital flows, legal frameworks, and academic scholarship to empower researchers, policymakers, journalists, and citizens with the data required to hold algorithmic systems accountable.
